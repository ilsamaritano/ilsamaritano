### 👨‍💻 About Me

I am a **PhD Student in Artificial Intelligence** at the **University of Pisa**, with a strong background in **Digital Humanities**. My research lies at the intersection of Cybersecurity, AI, and Data Science.

Currently, I focus on **Security Twins**, **Synthetic Data Generation**, and **Proactive Cyber-Resilience**. I leverage my hybrid background to approach complex problems with both technical rigor and analytical depth.

- 🔭 **Current Position:** PhD Student & Research Fellow at *Computer Science Dept. - University of Pisa*.
- ✈️ **Visiting:** Visiting PhD Student (VSRP) at **KAUST** (Saudi Arabia) - *Jan–Jun 2026* · Project: **ResilientGuard** (UAV Swarm Security · TinyML/Edge AI).
- 🎓 **Education:** Master's in Digital Humanities (110L) | Bachelor's in Digital Humanities.
- 💡 **Interests:** Cyber-Physical Systems, NLP, GDPR Compliance, TinyML, Byzantine-resilient Consensus.
- 📊 **Metrics:** 37 Citations · H-Index 4 · 23 Publications
